  - You have an account and are logged into the [Elements Console](https://console.scaleway.com)

  - You have [created at least one snapshot](/compute/instances/how-to/create-a-snapshot).

</Message>


## How to create an image from a snapshot? 

1. Log into your [Elements Console](https://console.scaleway.com) account. 
2. Click **Instances** in the menu on your left. The **Instances** page displays. 

    <Lightbox src="scaleway_instances_overview.png" alt="" />

3. Click the **Snapshots** tab. A list of your snapshots displays. 

    <Lightbox src="scaleway_list_snapshots.png" alt="" />

4. Click <Icon name="more"/> next to the snapshot from which you want to add an image.
