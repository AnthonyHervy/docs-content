title: How to create an image from a snapshot
description: This page explains how to create an image from a snapshot
dates:
  validation: 2021-08-05
  posted: 2021-08-05
---

<Message type="requirement">

  - You have an account and are logged into the [Elements Console](https://console.scaleway.com)
  - You have [created an Instance](/compute/instances/how-to/create-an-instance) 
  - You have [created at least one snapshot](/compute/instances/how-to/create-a-snapshot)

</Message>


1. Log into your [Elements Console](https://console.scaleway.com) account. 
2. Click **Instances** in the menu on your left. The **Instances** page displays. 

    <Lightbox src="scaleway_instances_overview.png" alt="" />

3. Click the **Imagess** tab. A list of your images displays. 

    <Lightbox src="scaleway_list_images.png" alt="" />

4. Click the **Create an image**. The **Create a New Image** overview page displays. 
5. Click the availability zone in which you want your image to be created. 
6. Type in the name of your image 

    <Message type="note">

    - Your image can only contain alphanumeric characters and dashes.

    </Message>
     
7. Select the snapshot from which you want to create your image in the drop-down box. 
8. Click **Create a new image** to create your image. 

    <Lightbox src="scaleway_last_button.png" alt="" />


<Message type="tip">

- You can also create an image from the **Snapshots** tab by clicking <Icon name="more"/> next to the snapshot from which you want to add an image. Type in the name of your image, then click **Create image from snapshot**. 

<Lightbox src="scaleway_createimg_snapshot.png" alt="" />

</Message>
