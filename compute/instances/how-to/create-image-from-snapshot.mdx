  validation: 2021-08-05
  posted: 2021-08-05
---

<Message type="requirement">

  - You have an account and are logged into the [Elements Console](https://console.scaleway.com)
  - You have [created an Instance](/compute/instances/how-to/create-an-instance) 
  - You have [created at least one snapshot](/compute/instances/how-to/create-a-snapshot)

</Message>


1. Log into your [Elements Console](https://console.scaleway.com) account. 
2. Click **Instances** in the menu on your left. The **Instances** page displays. 

    <Lightbox src="scaleway_instances_overview.png" alt="" />

3. Click the **Snapshots** tab. A list of your snapshots displays. 

    <Lightbox src="scaleway_list_snapshots.png" alt="" />

4. Click <Icon name="more"/> next to the snapshot from which you want to add an image. A list of actions displays. 
5. Select **Image from snapshot**. 
6. Enter the name of your image 

    <Message type="note">

    - Your image can only contain alphanumeric characters and dashes.

    </Message>
     
7. Make sure the selected snapshot is the one you want to create an image from. 
8. Click **Create image from snapshot** to create your image. 

    <Lightbox src="scaleway_createimg_snapshot.png" alt="" />

9. Click the **Images** tab if you want to see your newly created image.
